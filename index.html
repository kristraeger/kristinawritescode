About

front end magician & product pioneer

I love building products. I have managed the development of numerous web and mobile products. From a Fortune 500 corporation to a Y-Combinator startup, I have been an influential contributor in bringing products to market that improve and enliven everyday life.

To enrich my contribution to this process, I decided to learn how to code. I quickly fell in love with the front end, building beautiful user experiences with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I combine my international experience in operations management and technical development to build front end experiences that excite users.
